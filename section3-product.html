The Product

A collar that monitors
what matters —
every second of every day.

The PetTagz collar is a sensor-embedded health monitoring device modeled after the proven technology used in commercial dairy cattle monitoring — adapted for companion dogs and connected to a smartphone app that makes the data instantly actionable.

12 Health & Behavior Metrics — Tracked Continuously
🏃

Movement & Activity

Daily activity levels and patterns compared to your dog's baseline

🌡️

Body Temperature

Continuous skin temp monitoring with fever and hypothermia alerts

❤️

Heart Rate

Resting and active BPM tracked against breed-specific normal ranges

🌬️

Respiratory Rate

Breaths per minute — elevated rate can signal pain, stress, or illness

📈

Heart Rate Variability

HRV as a stress and recovery indicator, trended over time

😴

Sleep Patterns

Sleep duration and quality — disrupted sleep is an early warning sign

🍽️

Eating Behavior

Jaw movement and neck posture detect feeding session frequency and duration

💧

Drinking Behavior

Head-down posture detects drinking sessions — dehydration flagged early

🐾

Scratching Behavior

Frequency and intensity tracked — excessive scratching alerts to skin issues

🐕

Posture Detection

Standing, sitting, lying, rolling — unusual posture patterns flagged

🔥

Calories Burned

Daily energy expenditure calculated from activity and weight profile

🔊

Barking Detection

Duration, frequency and intensity — distinguishes normal from distress barking

⚡ Hot-Swap Battery

10-day minimum battery life under full sensor load. The collar features a quick-swap replaceable battery — pop out the depleted battery and snap in a charged one in seconds, without removing the collar from your dog. Spare batteries sold separately.

📱 Open API

The PetTagz collar connects via Bluetooth and Wi-Fi to the owner app. An open data API allows full integration with our proprietary app — not a locked white-label system. OTA firmware updates pushed independently.

🛡️ Built Tough

IP67 waterproof rating. Designed for daily wear in all conditions. FCC and RoHS certified for the US market. Available in multiple sizes to fit all breeds.

Four-Color Alert System

Green — All Clear

All 12 metrics within normal range. Your dog is healthy and calm.

No action needed.

Blue — Worth Noting

Minor deviation from baseline detected. Low concern but worth monitoring.

e.g. Slightly elevated scratching, mild restlessness

Yellow — Check In

Moderate concern. Trend warrants attention or a vet conversation.

e.g. Elevated heart rate for 3+ hours, skipping meals

Red — Act Now

High concern. Owner and vet notified immediately.

e.g. Sustained fever, distress barking, labored breathing
