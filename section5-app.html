The App

Everything you need to know
about your dog — in your pocket.

The PetTagz owner app turns 12 streams of sensor data into a simple, clear picture of how your dog is doing right now. No charts to interpret. No guesswork. Just the information you need, when you need it.

Owner App Features
📊

Live Health Dashboard

All 12 metrics in one view, updated in real time. Color-coded status at a glance.

🔔

Instant Alerts

Push notifications the moment something changes. Four alert levels — from informational to urgent.

📈

Trend History

Daily, weekly, and monthly views so you can see patterns over time, not just snapshots.

🐾

Dog Profile

Breed, age, and weight calibrate your dog's normal ranges so alerts are personalized, not generic.

🔋

Battery Status

Monitor collar battery level and get notified when it's time to swap in a fresh charged battery.

🏥

Vet Sharing

One tap shares your dog's complete health record with your veterinarian before any appointment.

Veterinarian Connection

Your vet finally gets to see the whole story.

A wellness exam gives your vet twenty minutes with your dog, once a year. PetTagz gives them months of continuous health data before you even walk through the door — making every visit more informed and every decision more confident.

📋
Shared Health Records

Owner and vet see the same data. Trends, alerts, and history — all in one place.

🔒
Closed Loop — Your Vet Only

PetTagz is not a public telehealth platform. Your data is shared with your vet, and no one else.

⚡
Early Detection

Vets can spot developing issues in the data weeks before symptoms appear in person.
