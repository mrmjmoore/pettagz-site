How It Works

Three steps.
Complete peace of mind.

PetTagz is designed to disappear into your dog's daily life. Put it on once and the system does the rest — monitoring, learning, and alerting so you don't have to wonder.

01
🐕
Put on the collar

The PetTagz collar goes on like any collar. All 12 sensors activate automatically and begin building your dog's health baseline within the first 24 hours.

→
02
📱
Connect the app

The PetTagz app syncs via Bluetooth and Wi-Fi. Your dog's health dashboard updates in real time — heart rate, temperature, activity, sleep and more, all in one place.

→
03
🔔
Get alerted instantly

The moment any metric moves outside your dog's normal range, you get a color-coded alert on your phone. Green means all clear. Red means act now. Everything in between keeps you informed.

Setup Time

Under 5 minutes from box to live monitoring. No tools, no configuration, no technical knowledge required.

Works While You Sleep

PetTagz monitors around the clock — including overnight. Unusual patterns during sleep hours are flagged just like daytime alerts.

Shares With Your Vet

One tap shares your dog's complete health history with your veterinarian — giving them data they've never had access to before.
