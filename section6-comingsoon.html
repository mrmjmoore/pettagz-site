What's Coming

The collar is just
the beginning.

PetTagz is being built as a platform — not just a product. The collar and owner app launch first, with a growing ecosystem of features rolling out in phases. Here's what's on the roadmap.

Phase 1 — Launch 🚀

Smart Collar + Owner App

The core product. Everything you need to start monitoring your dog's health from day one.

  • 12 sensor health metrics
  • Four-color alert system
  • 10-day hot-swap battery
  • Real-time dashboard
  • Basic vet record sharing
Phase 2 — Expanding 🏥

Full Vet Integration + Smart Feeders

Deeper veterinary tools and the first third-party hardware integrations.

  • Full vet prescription workflow
  • Auto-refill scheduling
  • Offline vet app functionality
  • Smart feeder integration
  • Food intake volume tracking
Phase 3 — Platform 🌐

Distributor Network + Smart Bowls

Opening the platform to regional distributors and completing the hydration picture.

  • Distributor portal marketplace
  • Regional distributor onboarding
  • Smart water bowl integration
  • Full hydration tracking
  • Platform revenue expansion
🥣

Smart bowl integration — without making the bowl.

The collar tracks eating and drinking behavior. Smart feeders and water bowls track actual volume consumed. In Phase 2 and 3, PetTagz integrates with leading smart bowl brands via API — pulling food and water intake data directly into your dashboard alongside collar metrics. PetTagz becomes the central health hub, not just a collar company.
